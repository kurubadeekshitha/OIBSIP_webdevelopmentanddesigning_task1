Our Story

Founded in the lavender fields of Provence in 1987, Dreamy Mist has been crafting ethereal fragrances that capture the essence of dreams and wonder.

Each perfume is carefully composed using the finest natural ingredients sourced from around the world, from Himalayan mist to Japanese cherry blossoms.

Our master perfumers blend tradition with imagination to create scents that transport you to a world of serenity and beauty.
